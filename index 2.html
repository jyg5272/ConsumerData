About this Application

This web application, operating under the name of “Informed Community”, will act as a tool to deliver relevant information, available from certain government agencies, to the site’s user. This information, sorted by zip code, will provide the user information from two specific areas covered by multiple U.S. government agencies – these two categories are business and environmental. Such data is often overlooked, hard to locate, or a burden to navigate due to size or complexity. The problem, which our application is poised to resolve, is that many Americans may not be aware of the information available through the relatively new Open Data project, nor are they aware of how to access it. To further exemplify the problem, this data is often collected and made available in its “raw” form. The data is made available on databases which without the necessary API calls, the data may not be found through traditional search

The Data

The data in this project is part of a U.S. Federal Government initiative to promote transperancy within government agencies, and to make their collected data useful to developers for future applications.
